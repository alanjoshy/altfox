Key Features

Live Reflection of Activities

As the cashier enters transaction details, both their physical actions and the transaction screen are visibly reflected on the surveillance camera feed.

Automated Greetings

The surveillance system is equipped with an automated greeting feature, providing voice-based or on-screen welcome when a customer or cashier is detected.

Fraud Detection

In cases where the cashier deletes a product from the transaction, the system marks it with a distinct '**' as a digital flag for potential manipulation.

Real-Time Event Alerts

Instant notifications for critical events to keep you informed and in control

Item Deletion Alert

If staff deletes a line item while serving the customer, the video records that specific moment and sends it to the owner live.

All Items Deleted Alert

If staff deletes all items during a transaction, the system captures and forwards the video of that moment to the owner live.

Refund Process Alert

If a refund is processed, the video of that exact moment is recorded and sent to the owner live.

TILL Closing Alert

At the end of the day, during the TILL closing process (which includes money counting and generating the Z report), the video is recorded and sent to the owner for review.
